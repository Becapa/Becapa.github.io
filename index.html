HELLO WORLD

=======

A long, long time ago, in a galaxy far, far away...

It is a period of unrest. In a stunning move, Emperor TheDonald has taken control of the Presidency and the Senate. Amidst all of the chaos and frenzy there was a sly smuggler girl named Trin Solo. She had a trusty side-kick woorgie named Arielbacca.

They were minding their own business on the desert planet of Tatooine, just walking around, when they happened upon a couple of nerf-herding, Packer-backing, Jedi-wannabes (one a chick, and one a dude) that were trying to plant a seed.

Trin Solo figured she should point it out to them, "Tatooine is a desert planet. The soil here is not fertile for planting seeds." Arielbacca agreed with a bark.

Not knowing what else to do and seeing Trin Solo's ship, the Millenium Falcon, off in the distance the chick, Kelsi-wan Kenobi, asked Trin Solo and Arielbacca if they could help them find a planet where their seed would grow.

"Well, it'll be a very difficult and treacherous journey, but yes! We'll help!" Trin Solo replied.

The dude, Mike Skywalker, humbly offered his gratitude, "We don't have any idea what might grow from this seed, but we can't thank you enough."

And so the crew, now of size four, left Tatooine in search of a fertile planet.

They tried Coruscant and Naboo... Endor, then Yavin... Hoth and Kashyyyk. Finally, they found the most fertile planet they could... Negaunee.

They planted their seed in Negaunee where it is slowly growing into something beautiful this very day.

And then, of course, they all lived happily ever after - together forever.

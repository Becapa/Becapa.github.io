There was a sly smuggler girl named Trin Solo. And she had a loyal side-kick woorgie named Arielbacca.

They were minding their own business one day on the desert planet of Tattoine. Just walking around, when they happened upon a couple of nerf-herding, Packer-backing, Jedi-wannabes (one a chick, and one a dude) that were trying to plant a seed.

Trin Solo figured she should point something out to the couple. Arielbacca agreed with a bark.

Not knowing what else to do and seeing Trin Solo's ship, the Millennium Falcon, off in the distance the chick, Kelsi-wan Kenobi, asked Trin Solo and Arielbacca if they could help them find a planet where their seed would grow.

"Well, it'll be a very difficult and treacherous journey, but yes! We'll help!" Trin Solo replied.

The dude, Mike Skywalker, humbly offered his gratitude, "We don't have any idea what might grow from this seed, but we can't thank you enough."

And so the crew, now of size four, left Tattoine in search of a fertile planet.
